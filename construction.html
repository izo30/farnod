  • Lamu Shopping Mall Complex

    The ongoing project involves completing a mega building that will house a shopping mall at Lamu town. The complex will host several shops including hotels, supermarkets and banks. This project provides employment opportunities to Lamu residents as a way to get the community involved.

  • Nairobi Underground Pathway

    The pathway between two corporates building will enable an easy and safe route between employees of the corporate. The pathway is large enough to include two lanes and will be open to the public as well. This will help reduce time taken by the corporate employees to cross the road as they move in between their corporate buildings.

  • Sendy Office Complex

    The new Sendy office complex will host offices for the ever growing Sendy Limited. The building will include offices, dining areas, hostels and conference rooms for the Sendy employees. The complex will hold offices for more than a thousand employees. It will be an environmental friendly complex as it has many trees and a large garden will be built as part of the complex.
